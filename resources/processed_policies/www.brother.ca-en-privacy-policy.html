  • Our Commitment To Privacy

    Your privacy is of great importance to us. Brother Canada ("Brother Canada", "we", "us" or "our") respects the privacy of our customers and visitors to the www.brother.ca website (the "Site"). The purpose of this Policy is to explain how we collect, use and disclose the personal information of our customers and Site visitors. For the purpose of this Privacy Policy, "personal information" means information about an identifiable individual.

  • Information Collection Personal Information You Provide

    In general, you can visit our Site without providing any personal information. However, in order to use certain features of our Site, you will need to provide personal information. We may also collect personal information when you contact us by telephone phone, mail or email.

    The personal information we collect includes:


    1. Name, address, e-mail address, phone number, and other contact information. We may collect some or all of this information when you create an account on our Site, check out as a guest on our Site, register for one of our programs, make a purchase, register a product with us, or contact us with questions or inquiries.
    2. Product, support and warranty information. This includes the products that you have purchased and/or registered with us as well as information you may provide when you contact us for support or in connection with your warranty.
    3. Payment information, such as credit card details, that we require in order to process your purchases.

    Information Collected by Automated Means

    Various technologies are used on our websites to make them more user-friendly and effective. The data collected by such automated means generally does not contain personal details about the user, but may include information such as pages accessed, number of visitors to the site and IP addresses. Examples of such technologies are cookies, web analytics and social media widgets, described below.


    Cookies & Web Analytics

    In order to improve our Site, we may use "cookies" or other tools to track your visit. A cookie is a small amount of data that is transferred to your browser by a web server and can only be read by the server that gave the cookie to you. One of the primary purposes of cookies is to help the website recognize you, including by recalling your specific information and settings, to deliver relevant content and to help ease website navigation.
    We and our service providers may use cookies to customize the user experience (such as setting the preferred language) and serve you content (such as advertisements, promotions, products or key messages) that we believe are relevant to you and your interests, based on your visits to our Site and third party websites. We and our service providers may also use cookies and web-analytics to collect information about your use of the Site and enable us to improve the way it works. We do not have access to or control over the use of cookies or web-analytics by our partners, affiliates, tracking utility companies and service providers.
    You may be able to set your browser to decline cookies or notify you before a cookie is set. However, if you decline cookies, you may not be able to use some of the features of the Site.
    In addition to cookies, the Site automatically logs the IP/Internet address of your computer, your browser version, referring URLs and general web visitor information used by our web platform and traffic reporting tools. If you are logged in to your account when you visit the Site, this web analytic information may be linked to your personal information, and your navigation around the Site may be tracked. We use this information to understand how the Site is being used so that we can improve the Site and offer appropriate products and services to our customers.


    Social Media Widgets

    Our Site may include Social Media Widgets, such as the "Share this" button or interactive mini-programs that run on our Site. These Widgets may collect your IP address, which page you are visiting on our Site, and may set a cookie to enable the Widget to function properly. Social Media Widgets are either hosted by a third party or hosted directly on our Site. It is your choice to use these Social Media Widgets, and your interaction with these Widgets is governed by the privacy policy of the company providing the widget.


    Device Data

    When you use a product that is connected to the Internet, we collect information about the product including model name and version of the product and browser, browser settings, language and version of the firmware, product usage information (for example, print volumes and which apps are used), serial number and IP address of the product, access logs of Brother websites and apps and anonymous log data ("Device Data"). We use Device Data, and may share Device Data with our parent company Brother International Limited ("BIL"), for the purposes of analyzing general product usage, improving products, developing new products and functions, and enhancing user experiences. We and our affiliates may also use Device Data in combination with cookies and similar technologies to serve you with content and advertising that we believe may be of interest to you. Our collection of Device Data is enabled by the software embedded in our products. You may opt-out of the collection of Device Data by following the instructions in the End User License Agreement for the software.

  • Use Of Personal Information

    We may use and combine the information that we collect in order to:

    • Provide the service or carry out the transaction you requested or authorized
    • Process your orders, including processing payments and delivering products
    • Administer Brother Canada programs, such as Brother Care
    • Provide timely, reliable services to our customers
    • Customizing your experience and the content you receive, including on websites and mobile apps
    • Keep you informed about information and offers that you might be interested in including contests, promotions and special pricing offers
    • Establish and maintain our relationship with you and provide on-going service(s) and customer support, including administering product warranties
    • To improve our products and services
    • Develop, implement, market and manage services for customers, including through the use of surveys
    • Assist in administrative functions, such as maintaining business records, to collect unpaid debts, for credit card processing, and to protect the business interests of Brother Canada and its customers
    • Protect you and us against error and fraud
    • Comply with legal requirements
      We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above. In some cases, we may be required by law to retain information for a longer period.
  • Disclosure Of Personal Information

    Brother Canada may share your personal information with third party service providers engaged to provide certain services on our behalf, such as shipping, payment processing, communications, marketing and analytics and customer service. These service providers are strictly prohibited from using your personal information for any purpose other than to provide these services to Brother Canada and are required to protect your personal information using security measures comparable to those used by Brother Canada. Due to business requirements and as permitted under applicable law, your personal information may be stored or processed outside of Canada by Brother Canada or by one of its third party service providers, including in the United States and Japan, in which case it is subject to the laws of that foreign jurisdiction and may be accessible to that jurisdiction’s government, courts or law enforcement or regulatory agencies.


      We may disclose personal information:

    • to our affiliates (including outside of Canada) for internal audit, management, billing or administrative purposes.
    • as necessary to meet legal, regulatory, insurance, audit, and security requirements.
    • to parties connected with the proposed or actual financing, securitization, insuring, sale, assignment or other disposal of all or part of Brother Canada or our business or assets, for the purposes of evaluating and/or performing the proposed transaction. Our successor or assigns may use and disclose your personal information for similar purposes as those described in this Privacy Policy.
    • and as otherwise with your consent or as permitted or required by law.
  • Consent

    We generally collect, use or disclose your personal information with your consent, or as allowed or required under the law. Your consent may be expressed in writing or be implied, and you may give it to us electronically, verbally (over the telephone), by mail or in person.

    It is always your choice whether or not you provide us with your personal information and you may decline to give us your personal information or withdraw your consent to our collection, use or disclosure of your personal information at any time, subject to legal and contractual restrictions and reasonable notice. However, a decision to withhold certain personal information or withdraw consent may restrict or prevent Brother Canada from providing goods and/or services to you in a particular way, such as fulfilling an online order, which requires personal information.

    If you wish to unsubscribe from our promotional email communications, simply click on the unsubscribe link in the footer of the email.

  • Security Of Your Personal Information

    We take steps to protect the security of your personal information by using a variety of security technologies and procedures to help protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. For example, the file containing your personal information is stored on our servers (or those of our service providers) and access to the file is limited to authorized employees and service providers. Sensitive personal information (such as credit card number) transmitted over the Internet is protected through the use of Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption or other technologies. Access to your account is available through a password and a unique customer ID selected by you. Your password is encrypted. We recommend that you do not divulge your password to anyone.

    No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, however. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. If you have any questions about security on our Web site, you can contact us at privacy@brother.ca.

  • Third-Party Sites

    You may be able to access third party web sites (including other Brother web sites) through links available on our Site. You understand and agree that your use of third party sites will be governed by the privacy policies of those web sites and not by this Privacy Policy. The privacy policies of other web sites may differ from this Privacy Policy to reflect differences in site features, company services or applicable law.
