RIVA PRIVACY POLICY

2 Expect, LLC. and its group companies (“2 Expect”, “RIVA Audio”, “RIVA”, “we”, “us” and “our”) are committed to protecting the privacy of our website visitors, customers, business partners, and end users. 2 Expect and RIVA provide service to users throughout the world.2 Expect is the data controller for your information with the exception of processing activities in relation to product support, product analysis and development activities outside of the United States of America as supplied by our authorized third parties. We will be clear about the data we collect and why we are collecting it. We protect your data and will not sell it to third parties.

The purpose of this privacy policy statement is to explain the type of information RIVA collects, either directly or indirectly, how we collect it, what we use it for and how users (each, a “User”) can restrict the use or disclosure of personally identifiable information collected
(1) when you visit a RIVA-controlled website (“Website(s)”),
(2) when you use any/all products offered by RIVA (RIVA product(s), including our RIVA apps (‘RIVA Audio app’, RIVA WAND app’, ‘RIVA Turbo app’, ‘RIVA app’ or ‘app(s)’),
(3) while involved in an offline interaction including a RIVA event (collectively referred to as, ‘event(s)’) or
(4) when you speak with RIVA Customer Support.

The purpose of collecting your data is to improve your experience with RIVA products, apps, websites and our renowned customer support team so you can enjoy your favorite music even more.

WHAT INFORMATION WE COLLECT ABOUT YOU

Information You Volunteer: We collect information you voluntarily provide us when you register a RIVA Product on our Websites, place an order, subscribe to our newsletter or RIVA Nation, respond to a survey or fill out a form.

Information We Collect Automatically: We automatically collect information about you, such as usage or functional data about your use of RIVA products or information collected by using cookies and similar technologies when you use, interact with or access us via our Websites or apps.

We may collect non-personal identification information about Users whenever they interact with our website or apps. Non-personal identification information may include the browser type, the type of device, install or uninstall of our apps and technical information about Users means of accessing our Websites and apps, such as the operating system and version, the Internet service providers utilized, webpages you have viewed on our Websites, IP address, general geographic location as indicated by the IP address, and how you interact with content on our Websites and apps. Sometimes we, or our service providers, may use the information we collect to try to locate similar unique users across multiple browsers or devices, such as smartphones, tablets, or computers, or work with providers who do this, in order to better tailor their content and provide a seamless experience across devices. If you wish to opt out of cross device tracking for the purpose of interest-based advertising, you may do so through your Android or Apple device-based settings.

Information We Collect From Other Sources: RIVA’s Websites may contain links to other websites that are operated by third parties. RIVA does not share your personal information with those websites, nor are we responsible for the privacy practices employed by websites linked to or from our Websites. In addition, these sites or services, including their content and links, may be constantly changing. These sites and services may have their own privacy policies and customer service policies. Browsing and interaction on any other website, including websites which have a link to our Websites, are subject to that website’s own terms and policies.

Connected RIVA products: If you connect your RIVA products to the Internet or a RIVA app, we may automatically receive information from our Products about how you use them. This information includes:

  • Technical data, such as your product serial number or model number, device operating system version, MAC address(es), Wi-Fi® signal strength, firmware version, IP address, SSIDs, log files, durations, service tokens, Internet connection type and carrier, and unique identifiers associated with the computer, tablet, or mobile device used in connection with the RIVA product
  • Usage data, such as time spent using different features/settings of the Product, the day and time you used the Product, button presses, the media and other external sources to which you connect your product, and, as applicable, your product’s power spectrum, sound pressure level, volume levels, and streaming information, time zone, and transactional data enabling digital rights management (e.g., for music content providers to give appropriate credit to artists depending on how often their content is played)
  • Service “tokens” containing authentication information that enables delivery of third-party content to your RIVA product (e.g., to synch your connected Products to your Spotify account each time you use them)
  • Diagnostic data, such as battery life, the quality of the wireless connection, and error logs); and
  • Environmental data (e.g., noise level and audio frequencies).

CHILDREN’S PRIVACY

Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important to RIVA. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at our websites from those we actually know are under sixteen (16) without parental consent, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under sixteen (16). If we discover, following the notification or a parent or guardian or by other means, that a child under the age of sixteen (16) is registered on our site by using false information, we will cancel the child’s account and delete the child’s personally identifiable information from our records.

HOW WE USE THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT

When Users supply information about themselves for a specific purpose, RIVA uses the information for that purpose (such as to provide the information the visitor has requested or to consider a visitor for a particular job). When visitors use our website to order products, or to request information about them, we may use the individual information as we would use the same information obtained offline – for example, to evaluate your needs and contact you regarding additional products, information or services that we believe you may find useful. You have the right to choose not to receive this information by notifying us of your preference.

Disclosure of Personal Information: We do not sell individual information obtained on-line about our visitors or Users. And, we will not disclose the personal information you provide to us via the Websites to parties unaffiliated with RIVA unless you specifically authorize it, we believe it is reasonably required by law or we believe that disclosure is reasonably required to protect the safety of customers, employees or property.

Third Parties: RIVA uses a variety of third-party contractors to perform services such as website management and hosting, online product purchases and shipping, credit card processing and email and digital marketing. In such instances, your personal information may be shared with these agents or contractors but only for the purpose of performing services on behalf of RIVA and in accordance with this privacy policy. In addition, if RIVA gets acquired by another company, your personal information will, in most instances, be part of the assets transferred to the successor company.

Opt Out Opportunity: We will always give you the opportunity to “opt out” of receiving direct marketing information. This means we assume you have given us your consent to collect and use your information in accordance with this policy unless you take affirmative action to indicate that you do not consent, for instance by clicking or checking the appropriate option or box at the point of collection. In some cases, when applicable, we will provide you with the opportunity to “opt in.” This means we will require your affirmative action to indicate your consent before we use your information for purposes other than the purpose for which it was submitted.

Any of the information we collect from you may be used in one of the following ways:

  • To personalize user experience.
  • To improve our Website offerings based on the information and feedback we receive from you.
  • To improve customer service by more effectively responding to your customer service requests and support needs.
  • To process transactions.
  • To administer a contest, promotion, survey or other Website feature.
  • To send Users information they agreed to receive about topics we think will be of interest to them.
  • To send periodic emails.

We use the data we collect to deliver and personalize our communications with you. We may use third-party web analytics services such as Google Analytics in our marketing to collect and analyze usage information through cookies and similar technologies; engage in auditing, research and reporting; assist with fraud prevention and provide certain features to you. In addition to cookies, we may use small graphic images known as ‘pixels’ (also known as web beacons, clear GIFs, or pixel tags). We may use pixels in our email communications to you to help us to understand whether our email communication has been viewed. We also use third-party pixels (such as those from Google, Facebook, and other advertising networks) to help us provide advertising that is relevant to your interests.

To provide this ‘interest-based advertising’ with third-party parters, the parties combine non-personal data about your online activities collected over time, which help customize advertising we deliver to you. Examples of this may include a RIVA advertisement on a Google-owned page such as a search results page, Facebook, Instagram or YouTube, or on a site within Google’s advertising network. We do not share your personal information as part of this process. When necessary, we will obtain your consent to this process. At all times, you can opt out of these personalized ads from third-party advertisers and ad networks who are members of the Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) or who follow the Digital Advertising Alliance’s Self-Regulatory Principles for Online Behavioral Advertising by visiting the opt-out pages on the NAI websites and DAA websites. Opting out may make the ads you see across the internet less relevant to you.
