Privacy Policy

 

Welcome to Okaysou website. Okaysou.com (referred as “We”) knows that you care how information about you is used and shared, and we appreciate your trust that we will do so carefully and sensibly.

 

Personal Information Collection

We may collect your personal information, such as your name, email, and/or phone number, or third-party account (like Facebook) when you create an online or application account or subscribe to our communications. If you refuse to provide such information, you may be not able to use many of our features. We use the information that you provide to respond your requests and communication with you.

 

Personal Information Use

We do not sell, rent, or lease its customers lists to third parties. We collect and use your personal information to 1) process transitions you make through this website; 2) send periodic emails to inform company news, updates, related product or service information, including sending you updates to our terms and policies or other important messages from time to time; also send promotion communications we believe might be relevant to your interests; 3) verify your account details and activities; 4) permit your products and the services to access Third-Party Accounts. These Third-Party Accounts may allow you to share personal information through our Services, such as Facebook posts or pictures.

 

Payment Information

We use a third-party payment processor (such as Paypal) to assist in securely processing your personally identifiable payment information. When you place an order through the Services, the credit card information that you provide is encrypted and transmitted directly to Paypal or other payment methods. We do not store your credit card information and do not control and are not responsible for Paypal or its collection or use of your information. You may find out more about how Paypal stores and uses your credit card information by accessing Paypal’s privacy policy.

 

Use of Cookies

We may use “cookies” to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you. One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you enter text on the Okaysou’s site, a cookie helps Okaysou to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as email addresses, phone numbers and so on. When you return to the same website, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the features that you customized. You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the Okaysou services and/or website.

 

Under-Age Use

About the protection of juveniles’ personal information. We attach great importance to the protection of juveniles’ personal information. If you are a juvenile under the age of 16, you must obtain written consent from your parent or legal guardian before using this product and service.

 

Opt-Out & Unsubscribe

We respect your privacy and give you an opportunity to opt-out of receiving announcements of certain information. Users may opt-out of receiving any or all communications from Okaysou at any time by clicking “unsubscribe”. You can also contacting customer service at support@okaysou.com if you are unable to unsubscribe.

 

Changes to Privacy Policy

Okaysou reserves the right to revise and update this Privacy Policy from time to time. All changes are effective immediately when we post them and apply to the Services thereafter. Your continued use of the Services after the time the changes are posted will indicate your acceptance of those revised changes, so please check this Privacy Policy periodically for updates.
