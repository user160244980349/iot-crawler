This Cookies Policy takes part in the Privacy Policy of the website hosted under the domain www.wavelt.com (the "Website") and under any subdomains or websites dependent on it. We use cookies to improve our services.

1. WHAT IS A COOKIE?

A cookies is a small information file created by the website you are visiting, which keeps information in the User’s computer to provide access to different features.

2. WHY DO WE USE COOKIES?

We use cookies to be able to obtain more information about the way Users use our content and help us improve their experience when visiting our Website.

3. WHAT KIND OF INFORMATION DO COOKIES PROVIDE WITH?

In particular, our cookies provide us with the following information:

(i) The provider’s domain name number, which grants you Internet access. For example, if your provider is XXX we are only going to identify him as xxx.es. By doing this, we can elaborate statistics about the countries and servers that visit our website more frequently.

(ii) The date and time of access to our Website and the amount of time every User spends on it. That allows us to find out the hours of most user influx, and make the necessary adjustments to avoid saturation problems.

(iii) The Internet address that provided the link to our Website. Thanks to this information, we can find out the effectiveness of the different banners and links that redirect to our server, with the purpose of boosting those that offer better results, and to satisfy the information needs of our Users.

(iv) The amount of daily visitors to every content. That allows us to know which areas the most successful areas and lets us to be able to boost and improve its content, with the purpose of giving more satisfactory information to the Users. The information obtained is totally anonymous, which means that it cannot be linked to a particular or identified User.

(v) The chosen language for every User during his first visit to the Website and to the restricted areas.

(vi) Security elements that intervene in access controls to restricted areas.

(vii) User’s activity in the Website: links and places where he clicks, pages visited, what he searches, etc. That allows us to improve the website features and to make it more attractive and useful for the User.

The information to which we have access through cookies lets us customize our content based on User’s interests, but in any case, our cookies do not let us obtain personal data, neither read data or other cookies existing in the User’s computer.

4. TYPES OF COOKIES

We use the following types of cookies in our Website:

According to the entity in charge to manage them:

(i) Own cookies: cookies sent to the User’s terminal devices from an device or domain managed by the editor which is the one that offers the services requested by the User.

(ii) Third party cookies: cookies sent to the User’s terminal device from a device or domain managed by the editor, or from another entity that is in charge of the processing the obtained data from cookie traces.

According to the time that they remain active:

(i) Session cookies: cookies designed to gather and store data while the User accesses a website. They expire once the User leaves the website.

(ii) Persistent cookies: cookies designed to gather and store data. The stored data are kept in the terminal, and are accessible and processed during the period of time defined by the cookie manager, it being such period of time may last from minutes to years.

According to their purpose:

(i) Technical cookies: these cookies let the User navigate throughout the Website and use its different options and services.

(ii) Analysis cookies: these cookies allow to quantify the amount of Users and their connection time, in order to develop appropriate statistical analysis and measurements about the usage of the offered services for the purpose of being able to improve the services provided to the Users.
