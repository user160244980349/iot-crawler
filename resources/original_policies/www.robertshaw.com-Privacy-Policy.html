    Robertshaw uses your personally identifiable information to help provide the highest level of customer service to our prospects, customers, suppliers and other parties. Personally identifiable information is collected to allow Robertshaw to better understand your needs, communicate with you, contact you for market research, provide marketing information or other materials we think would be of particular interest to you, help you complete a transaction, and for any other purposes identified when the personal information is collected.     Cookies and Other Passive Tracking Devices
    Robertshaw uses cookies, web beacons and IP addresses to generate aggregate and anonymous statistics about the website and to tailor the content of the website to make it more effective. Some websites will not allow you to complete certain activities if cookies are disabled. Robertshaw may combine the information it collects passively with your personal information.

    Transfer of Personally Identifiable Information
    Robertshaw will not sell, rent, transfer or lease your personally identifiable information to any third party, except in the following circumstances:

Accordingly, if you are located in a jurisdiction outside of the United States, your use and entry of personally identifiable information into this website constitutes your consent to the transfer of your personally identifiable information into the United States.

    Security
    Robertshaw takes appropriate physical, electronic and managerial procedures to protect the security of your personal information. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot ensure the security of the information you transmit. We recommend you take every precaution in protecting your personal information when you are on the Internet. For example, change your passwords often, use a combination of letters and numbers when creating passwords, and make sure you use a secure browser.
