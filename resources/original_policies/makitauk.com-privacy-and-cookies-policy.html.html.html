PRIVACY AND COOKIES POLICY

For our Form processing policy, click here

The protection of your privacy is important to us. We consider data protection a matter of trust and approach it with all seriousness. In our role as a data controller, we are responsible for any personal information you submit to us. We collect your personal data only where necessary for the purposes of our business activities, and we use the data only as specified in this Privacy Policy. Please read the Privacy Policy carefully before submitting any personal data to Makita (UK) Ltd.. The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to enable you, the user, to be confident that we, in our capacity as controller of personal data, are processing the information about you in accordance with applicable privacy legislation.

You may freely visit and browse our website without having to disclose any personal data to us.

  1. DATA CONTROLLER

Makita (UK) Ltd. is the controller of personal data for the processing of personal data through our digital channels, such as this website, mobile apps, and/or other future digital contact interfaces.

Makita (UK) Ltd. Michigan Drive, Tongwell, Milton Keynes. MK15 8JD

  1. REASON AND LEGAL BASIS FOR DATA PROCESSING

Makita collects, stores and uses your personal information as a data controller (“Register”) in connection with and in order to provide and develop Makita’s products, services and websites (together “Services”) according to this Privacy Policy. Makita collects personal data to improve existing customer relations, to acquire new customers and to send product personal data and marketing material including newsletters. The legal basis for processing your personal information is consent, to manage existing contractual relationships and to pursue the legitimate interests of our business to send you offers, updates or information on our products and services.

  1. SOURCE OF THE DATA

The Register is made up of Makita (UK) LTD’s customer register, which is updated through the three-year warranty sign-up form, the mailing list sign-up form and the survey/competition form found on our website.

Warranty Extension Service:

If you sign up your product for Makita (UK) Ltd.’s three-year warranty extension scheme, we will collect, store and process your personal data for the purposes of a possible warranty process as well as to send you relevant product information and Service offers.

Within the three-year warranty extension scheme, retailers of Makita (UK) Ltd. have the right to access Makita (UK) Ltd.’s warranty registration system to view essential information about the customer, provided that the customer has been linked to the retailer concerned in connection with the product registration. Retailers may use such data exclusively for purposes related to Makita’s three-year warranty extension.

You can view your personal data by logging in to your user account at www.makitauk.com. In your user account, you can view your registered products and modify your contact details and mailing list preferences. You are required to keep your login details confidential and to refrain from disclosing them to unauthorised third parties. We are not responsible for any unauthorised password use.

 Parcel shipping service:

If you use our parcel shipping service to send your product for servicing by Makita (UK) Ltd., we will collect, store and process your personal data for the purposes of the shipping process as well as to send you relevant product information and Service offers.

Within the parcel shipping service scheme, Makita (UK) Ltd.’s shipping service providers have the right to view essential information about the customer. Shipping service providers may use such data exclusively for purposes related to the provision of this service.

Mailing list and other usage:

By creating a user account, you can also sign up for Makita’s mailing list. This enables us to send you information about our websites, products, campaigns, newsletters and business partners. If you do not want to receive communications described above, you are free to cancel your mail subscription at any time.

  1. THE RECIPIENTS OF THE PERSONAL DATA

Makita may collect the following personal data for the Register:

  • name of the company, first name, surname, gender, address, postcode, postal area, email address and information relating to their employer, leisure activities or ownership of products
  • personal data obtained through the three-year warranty sign-up also include information about the purchased product, the serial number, the location of the product, as well as the place of purchase.

Sensitive personal data is not being processed

Sensitive personal data includes data which reveals your race or ethnicity, political views, religious or philosophical beliefs, or membership in a trade union, as well as personal data regarding your health or sex life (hereinafter referred to as “Sensitive Personal Data”). Data regarding health may include, for example, illness -related job absence, pregnancy, and doctor visits.

We do not process any Sensitive Personal Data within the scope of the Services.

  1. TRANSFER OF DATA TO A THIRD COUNTRY OR INTERNATIONAL ORGANISATION

Makita does not share your personal data without your consent unless required by the authorities and by law. Personal data is stored and processed automatically within the European Union. All maintenance, processing and use of personal data is done solely by Makita (UK) Ltd..
