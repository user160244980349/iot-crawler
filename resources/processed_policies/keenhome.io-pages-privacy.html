What Information Does Keen Home’s Website Collect?

Keen Home collects certain personal information at keenhome.co or keenhome.io in several ways and from several sources. Specifically, Keen Home’s website collects the following information:

  • Personal Information: If you pre-order or purchase Keen Home products from our website, we may ask you to create a Keen Home Account, and to provide certain information including your name, email address, shipping address, payment card information, and other information necessary to complete a transaction. Keen Home calls this information “Personal Information.” Although you may be asked to enter your payment card number when pre-ordering or ordering goods and services, Keen Home does not collect, process, store, or retain payment card information. All payment card information is collected, stored, and processed by Keen Home’s payment card processing Partner—Shopify, Inc. If you do not want to share your personal information with Keen Home, please do not pre-order or order goods and services, or create a Keen Home Account.
  • Site Visit Information: Keen Home’s website passively collects certain information each time you visit our website. This may include information collected from cookies and other common activity logging technologies. This information includes your browser type, operating system, IP address, device specific data, certain search data, web-history data, and general statistics about your activities on Keen Home’s website.

Keen Home doesn’t sell or rent the Personal Information you share with us through our website to third-parties.

  • Keen Home’s use of your personal information: We use the Personal and Site Visit Information you provide, as well as other information, to process pre-orders and orders for goods and services, to contact you, to provide relevant advertisements, to ensure our website is working properly, and for other purposes that are consistent with the purpose for which you shared your personal information.
  • Keen Home may share your Personal and Site Visit information with its Partners: Keen Home may share your Personal and Site Visit Information with certain trusted third-parties to perform functions and provide services to us, including site hosting and maintenance, customer relationship, database storage and management, advertising, and direct marketing campaigns. We call these trusted third-parties our “Partners.” Keen Home carefully selects each Partner, and our Partners take your privacy and the security of the information we share with them seriously. In addition, all our Partners have signed contracts with Keen Home obligating them to securely store and use your personal information, and to respect your privacy.
  • Legal and law enforcement requests: Keen Home may also disclose your Personal or Site Visit Information as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, warrant, or similar legally enforceable request for information.

Does Keen Home Use Cookies?

Keen Home’s website uses cookies to improve your experience on our site, and to improve the relevance of the advertisements you receive. Keen Home uses persistent cookies. These cookies stay in your browser and allow us to recognize you when you return to the Site. Keen Home uses these cookies to better understand how you use our website, to provide you with more relevant advertisements, to diagnose and fix technical problems, and to otherwise enhance our services.

You can confirm, change or update the personal information you share with Keen Home by logging into your Keen Home Account or you can also ask Keen Home for help with changing or updating your information by contacting us at help@keenhome.io .

Keen Home may send you marketing messages, including marketing sent to you by email, about our innovative products, and to alert you to relevant advertisements and opportunities. Our website, and our products and services are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Keen Home does not permit children under the age of 13 to register on its website, to create accounts, or to pre-order or order goods and services through the website. If you are the parent or guardian of a child under 13, and you believe he or she has shared personal information with Keen Home, please contact us at help@keenhome.io and ask that the information been removed from Keen Home’s systems.

Does Keen Home Track My Activity Across Other Websites?

Like many websites, Keen Home uses select advertising Partners to “retarget” advertisements. Retargeting occurs when a select advertising Partner places a persistent cookie on your computer or mobile device to track your activities across other websites, and to improve the relevance of the advertisements you receive across the websites you visit.

Also, when you visit our website, Keen Home automatically collects the name of the website you came from (called the “referring page”), and the name of the website you visited immediately after ours (called the “exit page”). This information is used exclusively to improve your experience on Keen Home’s website, and to increase the relevance of the advertisements you receive.

Because Keen Home use persistent cookies, your selection of the “Do Not Track” option on your browser may not stop the cookies we use from collecting information about other websites you have visited. However, you may prevent our advertising Partners from tracking your activities by adjusting the settings in your browser so it rejects cookies or so it is set to “Do Not Track.” Please remember, Keen Home has no means of confirming or controlling whether its advertising Partners honor the “Do Not Track” browser settings.

An advertiser or third-party may receive the information you enter into a form or survey contained in an advertisement.
