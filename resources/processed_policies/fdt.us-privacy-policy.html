This means what?

FDT uses AWeber to responsibly manage it's email marketing campaign. FDT pledges to:

  • Abide by Aweber's anti-spam rules and anti-spam laws like CAN-SPAM
  • Allow you to unsubscribe whenever you like

How do I unsubscribe?

At the end of every email there will be an unsubcribe link.
Click on that link and then select the unsubscribe button.
Once submitted you will be immediately unsubscribed

How is spam prevented?

FDT uses Aweber Professional Email Marketing service which reviews FDT's list with a variety of automatic as well as manual tests against irresponsible mailing.
