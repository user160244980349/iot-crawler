According to the new Euro General Data Protection Regulation (“GDPR”), Bryton have this new Privacy Policy (“The Policy”) to improve our protection of End User’s privacy. Also follow the GDPR, Bryton disclosures the type of privacy information to our end user.     1. The Bryton clients’ information, the reasons to keep and users’ selection:

    • 1.1 Bryton GPS Cycling Computer(Product)
      User will have to input several information into a product when the first time using one. With the information the Product can identify and assist user to know his/her own status in exercise, user could have better control of his/her performance with a Product assisting. The information user may input into a Product includes Gender, Birth, Height, Weight, MHR, LTHR, FTP, MAP, Bike Weight and Wheel Diameter. The geographical information is also required, the Altitude is the very info usually needed. Product will collect the GPS signal automatically to record user’s moving track. The data from Speed, Cadence, Energy and Heart Rate sensor is also collected automatically, if there is correspondence sensor on the user’s bike. All the data will be used to convert into the display data on Product. All the information user input and data Product collected will be keep in the Product until the data is erased, re-write or renewed. Owner of a Bryton Product also an owner of privacy information in it, owner can add up, renew or erase information with the Product inner function. If an owner of a Bryton Product willing to accept free Bryton Cloud Service, which means to use the internet cloud backup free space offered freely by Bryton, will have to agree The Policy first. Once The Policy is agreed, a client can upload all his data in Product as a backup. A client can check the backup via Bryton internet website. To use the Product without Bryton free cloud service will be no differences, the cloud service is not a part of the Product.
    • 1.2 Customer service Information(Taiwan Only)
      Client has problem with his Product can come to Bryton website and ask for help. This process will require the client to fill in contact information, includes username, e-mail address, cell phone number, nation or location. When customer service personnel of Bryton answer the question will use the information that client leave on the website. All the discussion and the service feedback will be keeping on the customer service record and the client can have a serial number and link in the answering mail to re-check the record. One link can reach only one record refer to one case. After the client’s request is closed. The record of the very case can be removed by the customer service personnel with the client’s request to do so.
    • 1.3 The free Bryton cloud backup service
      During the using of a Product, the user’s data kept recording. The user’s information includes the data mentioned in The Policy section 1.1 and other collected from the sensor the user have. Bryton offer the free cloud service for clients to do the data backup. Client have his/her own rights to decide to use the free cloud service or not. A client can check his/her backup on the Bryton free cloud via Bryton APP or PC and delete them via Bryton App or the Bryon website. (https://active.brytonsport.com) The update of the backup data can be upload from the Product. To stop the data upload, client can simply set the upload function off.
    • 1.4 Bryton APP
      A client can easily acquire Bryton App from App store (for IOS) or google play (for Android). To use Bryton APP, client will have to input username and E-mail to register an account. Bryton App can help client use mobile device access bryton website. The information a client input into the APP, the information will automatically upload to the Bryton Cloud and combine with client’s backup. The same as section 1.3, the data can be easily checked by client through APP or via PC. A client can delete the file if he/she wants to.
    • 1.5 Client sent the information to other website
      For clients’ convenience, Bryton Product add up quick linking for user to transford to Strava, TrainingPeaks, SelfLoop or other user data upload website. However the information user select to transford to other website, Bryton take none responsibility. Clients will have to check the data privacy policy with the website they are using.
