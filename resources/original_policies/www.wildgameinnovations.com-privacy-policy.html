COOKIES AND REMARKETING

Through this website, we receive and store certain types of information automatically when you interact with us. This is generally not personally identifiable information, but if it is, it will be treated as such. This information includes Cookies, which include HTML Cookies. HTML Cookies are a small amount of data that is dent to your browser from a webserver and stored on your hard drive which provide a means for us to keep track of your online preferences and patterns. For example, HTLM Cookies enable us to identify returning users and customize your experience to better reflect your interests and preferences. We may also use Flash Cookies, which track a person’s internet use across multiple websites and are not deleted or controlled in the same way as HTML Cookies.

Our webserver may also automatically recognize or collect certain information from your computer, such as your domain name, IP address, type of computer, what web browser you are using and the city/state where you are located, your browsing history, the length of your visit, the pages you viewed, your ISP, certain software you may have (e.g. Java), and your computer’s language setting. An IP Address is assigned to your computer when you use the Internet.

We may use the information we collect for the purposes of system administration, to improve our websites and/or to customize your experience at our website. We may also use such information in the aggregate to analyze website traffic and usage.

The website uses remarketing features that may result in ads being targeted to you on third-party sites. No personally identifiable information will be shared with these third parties under any circumstances, but you may see ads for the website or products affiliated with the Site elsewhere on the Internet. Third parties (such as Amazon) may place and use pixels on the Site to tailor user experiences. Google Analytics may also be enabled.
