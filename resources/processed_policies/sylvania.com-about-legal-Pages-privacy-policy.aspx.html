Information Collected via Technology.
  • Automatically Collected. When you interact with our Websites or the SYLVANIA Mobile Apps, we may automatically collect certain technical information about your equipment, browsing actions, and patterns. That information may include Internet Protocol (“IP”) address (a number that is automatically assigned to your computer or phone when you use the Internet, which may vary from session to session), domain name, browser type, Internet service provider (“ISP”), mobile device information, product usage logs including turn-on, turn-off, timer, color temperature setting, scene setting, etc. We use this information to analyze trends and administer the Websites and the SYLVANIA Mobile Apps.
  • Cookies. Like many online services, we use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your visit to our Website. “Cookies” are small pieces of information that a website sends to your computer’s hard drive while you are viewing the website. We use Cookies to detect whether you have enabled javascript. You cannot opt out of this cookie and still use the Services.
