PRIVACY STATEMENT
(Effective Date: March 19, 2019)

Perfect Aire LLC, its subsidiaries and affiliates (collectively “Perfect”, “our”, “us”, or “we”), are committed to protecting the privacy users of our Perfect Aire website or mobile application (“app”). This Privacy Statement describes how we collect and use personal and other information about you from your use of the website/ mobile application and the rights you may have with respect to such information.

By downloading and using the App or website on your mobile phone/tablet device/desktop computer (“Device”) or accessing or using any other services we offer in connection with our products and services (collectively, our “Services”), you agree to allow us to collect and process information as described in this Privacy Statement.

This Privacy Statement applies to your use of our Services, which are authorized only in Canada and the United States of America.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT AND HOW WE COLLECT IT

To better provide you an efficient and customized experience we collect two types of information about our you: (a) Personal Information, and (b) Non-Personal Information.

Personal Information
Personal Information means any information or set of information that identifies or could be used by Perfect to identify you and includes, but is not limited to, your name, home or work address, phone number, email address and where your Device and Perfect Product is used. We collect Personal Information that you provide to us voluntarily when you contact us about your Perfect product Services; register your Perfect product; download, activate, set-up and use our App; and by choosing to allow location-based automation from your Device.

Non-Personal Information
When you use the App or website, we may collect certain information that, by itself or in combination with other information, does not identify you personally (“Non-Personal Information”). Non-Personal Information includes, but is not limited to, information about your Device and your use of the App or website, and about your Perfect product such as its location temperature, temperature settings, performance and operation. We also may collect data about the location of your Device and your Perfect Product.

We may combine your Personal Information and aggregated Non-Personal Information to improve the services provided by our App and website. If we combine any Non-Personal Information with your Personal Information, the combined information will be treated as Personal Information under this Privacy Policy for as long as it remains combined.

Children
Our Services are not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13 and we will not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under such age for any purpose, nor will we accept registration from such individuals.

HOW WE USE THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT

  • We may use your personal information to:
    Deliver services to you or carry out transactions you have requested, including, but not limited to, providing information on our products and services, registering your Perfect products, providing warranty and product support, and answering your communications.
  • Administer and protect the App and website, including troubleshooting, maintenance and support.
  • Perform business analyses or for other purposes that help us to develop and improve the quality of our business, products, website and App.
  • Conduct research to help us analyze purchasing preferences and measure the effectiveness of our advertising and marketing activities.
  • Inform you of our products or services which may be of interest to you unless you opt-out from such marketing communications sent via e-mail at any time, free of charge by using the “unsubscribe” link in any e-mail marketing materials you receive from us, or by contacting us using the information listed below.
  • Detect, prevent and respond to fraud, intellectual property infringement and violations of law.

HOW WE SHARE INFORMATION

We may share your Personal Information with our affiliated companies (“Affiliates”) to help us improve our App, website, products and services. Other than an Affiliate, we will not sell, license or share your Personal Information except to:

  • Our service providers, dealers, distributors, agents or contractors who are contractually restricted from using or disclosing your information except as necessary to perform services on our behalf or to comply with any legal requirements;
  • Refer you with a local dealer or installer upon your request;
  • Comply with legal obligations;
  • Investigate suspected or actual illegal activity, and prevent property and personal injury or financial loss; or
  • In connection with the sale or transfer of all or a portion of our business or assets.

HOW WE PROTECT INFORMATION

We take the security of your Personal Information seriously. We maintain physical, administrative, and technical safeguards designed to maintain the confidentiality and security of your Personal Information. However, we cannot guarantee the security of your Personal Information when you transmit to us your Personal Information through the internet or the App of if you circumvent of any security measures in the App or website.
