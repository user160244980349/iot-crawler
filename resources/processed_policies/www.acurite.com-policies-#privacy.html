Billing Policy

The maximum online order dollar amount is $2,000. To place an order over $2,000 please contact us via chat or email and we will help place the order for you.

Credit cards are authorized in real-time at the moment the order is placed on AcuRite.com. The card is charged when the order has shipped. Temporary holds may be placed on funds for failed orders due to mismatching billing information or other payment issues. This hold is released within 2-5 business days depending on the bank/card issuer and we do not control this time frame. The billing address provided to us must exactly match the one on file with the card issuer/bank or else the payment and order are rejected by the system.

Customer Reviews Submission Guidelines

We would love to hear from you! As an AcuRite customer, your product feedback is very important to us. Reviews help future shoppers make the right buying decisions and let us know ways we can continually improve the products we offer.

Once you’ve had a chance to set up and use your AcuRite product, we invite you to tell us about your experience with the product you purchased and share your thoughts with other customers. We also encourage you to attach an image or share a video link of the product in your environment to help illustrate how you use it. Some of our customers are extremely creative and we love seeing all the unique ways people install and use their weather stations!

Please note: If your review does not comply with our guidelines, it may be rejected. Also, in order to write a review, you need to have an Acurite.com account .

What to include in your review :

  • Always include the "why": The best reviews don’t just say whether or not you liked a product, but also why.
  • Be specific: Your review should be relevant to the product you’re reviewing. For video and image reviews, we recommend you write a brief introduction.
  • Be sincere: We welcome your honest opinion about our products. We do not remove reviews because simply because they are negative. We believe all helpful information can inform our customers’ buying decisions.

We will not accept the following feedback:

Reviews not about the product:

  • Shipping experience
  • Store experience
  • Product availability

Reviews containing inappropriate or offensive content:

  • Inappropriate language
  • Hate speech and offensive content
  • Promotion of illegal conduct

Further, if you are having a troubleshooting problem, we recommend you first contact us via chat or email. This will give us the chance to quickly solve any issue you may be experiencing. Typically, problems are easily rectified with simple solutions, such modifying placement of a sensor or display.

Additionally, we may reach out to you directly to offer support on any issues you may be experiencing.
