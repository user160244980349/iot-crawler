Privacy Policy

DUKAP® is committed to customer satisfaction, we respect your privacy and will only collect information pertinent to customers and their orders. This information will not be sold, rented, or shared with anyone other than the parties required to fulfill the orders. DUKAP® values your privacy as much as you do and vows to take every preventive measure to ensure its safety. This includes, but is not limited to, administrative oversight and control, restricting access to authorized personnel, using the latest in encryption protocol (SSL), and properly disposing of information by virtue of making it unreadable.
