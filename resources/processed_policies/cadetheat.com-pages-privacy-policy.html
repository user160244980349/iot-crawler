The information we collect
We do not sell, trade, give away or rent your personal information collected during the use of our Website without your permission.

We may collect and store personal information, such as your name, address, e-mail address, telephone and mobile number and social media ID to respond to questions, process orders or requests, register a product, order literature, enter contests, sign up for newsletters, participate in surveys or to provide customer service.

We may also retain information about your transactions with us, as well as information about our communications with you such as account history, requests for information and our responses.

In addition, we may automatically gather general statistical information about our Website and visitors, such as number of visitors, IP addresses, browsers, pages viewed, and products and services purchased. In doing so we do not reference you by individual name, address, e-mail address or telephone number. We aggregate this data and may provide this statistical information to third parties, but when we do so we do not provide personal information without your permission.

As part of our service, we may use "cookies" to store and, at times, track information about our site visitors. A "cookie" is a small file stored on your hard drive by your browser. Generally, we use cookies to remind us who you are and enable us to access your account information so you do not have to reenter it; gather statistical information about usage on our site, research visiting patterns, help target advertisements based on user interests; assist our partners track visits and process orders; and track progress and participation in promotions. Most browsers accept cookies automatically but allow you to disable them. Please consult your specific browser instructions to learn more about these features. In some instances, our partners and advertisers may from time to time appear at our site and may use their own cookies. We do not control these cookies and are they not subject to our Website.

Security
We employ reasonable and current security methods to prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure correct use of information.

You should take care with regard to how you handle and disclose your Personal Information or any username or password that you are required to use to access services on our Websites. Note that Cadet will not request sensitive personal information in our emails to you and you should verify the authenticity of any such request.

Please remember that any information you share in public areas, such as reviews, testimonials or feedback sections, becomes public. Our Policy does not apply to any information you choose to make public. Please be careful about what you disclose and do not post any personal information that you expect to keep private.

Note that the Internet has inherent security risks and while we try to protect your personal information, we cannot ensure or guarantee the security of any information you disclose to us, and you do so at your own risk.
