If in the case Naxa were to need and receive your credit card information, Naxa will not store or keep any of this information on their database nor with they share this information with any uninvolved 3rd  party. Depending on the 3rd party option provided and used the purchase after compeleted will generally be redirected to a Naxa completion page.  If in the case you are having trouble with this please contact Naxa directly at support@naxa.com
