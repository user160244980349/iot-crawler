Privacy Policy

Last modified: July 2020

Introduction

Milwaukee Electric Tool Corporation ("Company" or "We") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

This policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you visit the website www.milwaukeetool.com (our "Website"), when you use and access the One-Key program from the Website, when you install, access or use the ONE-KEY™ Mobile Application or other mobile and desktop apps you download from this Website, or apps provided by or on behalf of Company through an online app store (the "Apps"), or when you use certain Milwaukee tools and equipment that are ONE-KEY™ ready (“ONE-KEY™ Tool”), and our practices for collecting, using, storing, maintaining, protecting and disclosing that information.

This policy applies to information we collect:

  • Via the Apps.
  • On this Website (including use of the ONE-KEY™ program through the Website).
  • By using ONE-KEY™ Tools.
  • In e-mail, text and other electronic messages sent through or in connection with the Website and/or the Apps.

Please read this policy carefully to understand our policies and practices regarding your information and how we will treat it. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, your choice is not to use the Apps or our Website. By accessing or using the Apps and/or this Website, you agree to this privacy policy. This policy may change from time to time. Your continued use of the Apps and/or this Website after we make changes is deemed to be acceptance of those changes, so please check the policy periodically for updates.

Children

The Apps and our Website is not intended for children under the age of majority in their jurisdiction of residence. No one under the age of majority may provide any information to or on the Website or Apps. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a child, do not use or provide any information on or through the Apps; or on or through the Website. Additionally, do not make any purchases through the Apps or the Website, use any of the interactive or public comment features of the Apps or the Website or provide any information about yourself to us, including your name, address, telephone number, e-mail address or any screen name or user name you may use. If we learn we have collected or received personal information from a child without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information. If you believe we might have any information from or about a child, please contact us at 1-800-SAWDUST (1-800-729-3878).

  • Directly from you when you provide it to us.
  • Automatically when you use the Website, Apps and ONE-KEY™ Tools.

  • Tool inventory information.

The Apps also may access metadata and other personal information associated with other files stored on your device. This may include, for example, photographs, audio and video clips, personal contacts and address book information. You can manually decide to share such information and the Apps will access such stored information or files you select to use for various purposes in the Apps, including uploading such information to the Website

Automatic Information Collection And Tracking 

As you navigate through and interact with our Website, or when you download, access and use the Apps, we may use automatic data collection technologies. These technologies do not identify you as an individual, but do collect information regarding your internet service provider, computer, mobile device and connected tools. An example of such technology would be the use of browser cookies.

The type of such automatically collected information includes:

  • Usage Details. When you access and use the Apps, Website, or ONE-KEY™ Tools we may automatically collect certain details of your access to and use of the Website, ONE-KEY™ Tools or Apps, including traffic data, location data, tool connection and performance data, logs and other communication data and the resources that you access and use on or through the Apps or Website.
  • Computer Information. Information about your computer and internet connection, including your IP address, operating system and browser type.
  • Device Information. We may collect information about your mobile device and internet connection, including the device's unique device identifier, IP address, operating system, browser type, mobile network information and the device's telephone number.

If you do not want us to collect this information do not use the Website and do not download the Apps or delete them from your device.

Collection Of Information And Geo-Location By ONE-KEY™ Tools

The ONE-KEY™ Tools contain technology that identifies the serial number and type of the tool (saw, drill, etc.) and broadcasts this information as a “Notification” along with the tool’s geo-location to phones utilizing the ONE-KEY™ App in the surrounding area and to other internet enabled devices that the Company has authorized to be part of the ONE-KEY™ system (collectively “ONE-KEY™ Devices”). ONE-KEY™ Tools do not collect or store personally identifying information. Accordingly, Notifications do not contain personally identifying information. Instead, you can create an account and provide your personal information on our ONE-KEY™ App and link specific ONE-KEY™ Tools to your account on the ONE-KEY™ App. ONE-KEY™ App users may only access and view the Notification and geo-location information transmitted by those ONE-KEY™ Tools that they specifically link to their ONE-KEY™ App. All ONE-KEY™ Devices, however, monitor for and receive Notifications from any ONE-KEY™ Tool, even if the ONE-KEY™ Device is not specifically associated with that ONE-KEY™ Tool. The ONE-KEY™ Device will, from time to time, transmit any One Key Tool's geo-location and Notification information that it has detected and received to Company. Furthermore, no information regarding the ONE-KEY™ Device is provided to the ONE-KEY™ Tool.

The detection and transmission of Notification data by the ONE-KEY™ App may be disabled at any time by a feature on the ONE-KEY™ App. When disabled, the ONE-KEY™ App will not transmit the location of your phone or any Notifications or geolocation from any ONE-KEY™ Tools. The broadcasting by ONE-KEY™ Tools or their Notifications and geo-location, however, cannot be disabled or turned off.

Additionally, users of the ONE-KEY™ App or ONE-KEY™ program on the Website can manually link a ONE-KEY™ Tool to their “inventory” in their ONE-KEY™ user account (“User Inventoried ONE-KEY™ Tool”). Information concerning this User Inventoried ONE-KEY™ Tool (tool settings, tool location, serial number and other information) will be collected and stored by the ONE-KEY™ App and uploaded to the users account on the Website where it will be accessible to the user. The ONE-KEY™ App or ONE-KEY™ program on the Website can control the settings of the User Inventoried ONE-KEY™ Tool. Furthermore, a user can create a guest account (“Guest Account”) on the ONE-KEY™ App or the Website. A user can allow or disallow a Guest account User using the ONE-KEY™ App or ONE-KEY™ program on the Website to: connect to a User Inventoried ONE-KEY™ Tool and change the tools settings; connect to a User Inventoried ONE-KEY™ Tool and lock or unlock the User Inventoried ONE-KEY™ Tool; access a listing of all the One Key Tools in a users inventory; add or edit ONE-KEY™ Tools to a user's inventory. Note that the above features of the ONE-KEY™ App may not be available for all versions of the ONE-KEY™ App or for all ONE-KEY™ App tools.
