Your privacy is recognized as the most important for Sunba Technology Co., Ltd. Collection and Use of Information (Website Access)

You may be asked to provide a username, email, password and a name to uniquely identify yourself in this website with the following practices: manage your account, use the shopping cart, enter the forum or communicate with Sunba Technology. This information is only used to provide access to the website in the above practices. Sunba will not sell, share, or rent your personal information to any third party or use your e-mail address for unsolicited mail unless requested by law or with your consent.

Collection and Use of Information (Shopping)

Apart from your unique identifier on this website (a username, email, password and a name), to process transactions, sometimes additional information is requested by our 3rd party payment processing provider such as Paypal. Except for your email address, Sunba will only be using the information you provided such as product reviews, feedback, comments and encouragements as objective information to help us develop, deliver, and improve our products, services, content, and advertising.
