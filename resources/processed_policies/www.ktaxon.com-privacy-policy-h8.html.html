By using any of our web sites, mobile applications, or sharing your contact information with us, you are accepting and consenting to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. We collect information such as your name, phone number, billing information, shipping information, e-mail address and other personal information submitted when you are placing an order. Please note we do not store credit card numbers in our order system.
In addition, we may need to contact you via email questions or issues specific to your order, entry, etc., even if you have opted to not receive marketing communications from us.

Those third parties include authorized contractors, consultants and other companies working with us (agents). They only have access to personal information needed to perform their functions, and will not share any personal information with others or use it for any other purpose than improving our services and offerings.

Cookies

When you visit our web sites, we send one or more cookies to your computer or other devices. We may also use cookies in emails that you receive from us. A cookie is a small data file that is placed on the hard drive of your computer when you visit a web site. A session cookie expires immediately when you end your session (i.e., close your browser)
