REGISTER NAME

Unipro Oy Ltd customer and marketing register

THE PURPOSE OF THE PROCESSING OF PERSONAL DATA

The Unipro Oy Ltd customer and marketing register contains data on the contact persons of our customers, companies, organisations and partners and the contact persons of potential customers and partners. The personal data are used for the purposes of maintaining customer relationships and partnerships. The data are also used to manage contacts and information related to similar events concerning Unipro Oy Ltd’s website and services related to it.

DATA CONTENT OF THE REGISTER

In addition to company details, the register contains the following data on the companies’ contact persons: 1. Basic contact person details: • name, form of address • postal address, telephone numbers, email address and other contact details • title, position and role within the company. 2. Data concerning customer communications, marketing and other forms of communication, such as • mailing lists (customer magazine and newsletter, for example) • consents and prohibitions concerning direct marketing and other consents and prohibitions • data about sent offers, campaigns, meetings and other contacts and their results 3. Data concerning the customer relationship, agreements, orders and deliveries such as • acquired products and services • buyer details • offers, orders, agreements, invoicing, complaints, feedback and the log of other customer contacts

PERSONAL DATA RETENTION

The personal data are retained only for the period necessary for the purposes of performing the objectives defined in this privacy policy. Personal data are erased once the retention of the personal data in question is no longer required by law, Unipro Oy Ltd’s rights or obligations or the person’s rights or obligations. For instance, the personal data are erased without undue delay when a person requests the erasure of their data from the customer and marketing register. Due to technical reasons, the personal data are stored by Unipro Oy Ltd as back-up copies for up to a year before their erasure in such cases. Unipro Oy Ltd will endeavour to maintain the accuracy of the personal data it holds by erasing unnecessary data and updating obsolete data.

REGULAR SOURCES OF DATA

The identification and basic data in the Unipro Oy Ltd customer and marketing register are generated by Unipro Oy Ltd’s operations and retrieved from the customer or partner.

REGULAR DISCLOSURE OF DATA

The data in the Unipro Oy Ltd customer and marketing register are not disclosed to external parties. Selected personal data can be disclosed to Unipro Oy Ltd’s partners, for example, if Unipro Oy Ltd decides to outsource the processing of the customer magazine or other marketing material.

TRANSFERRING THE DATA OUTSIDE THE EU OR EEA

The data in the Unipro Oy Ltd customer and markeeting register are not transferred outside the EU or EEA.

PRINCIPLES OF THE REGISTER’S PROTECTION

A) Manual material The data are stored in locked and guarded premises and accessible only to designated people.

B) Automatically processed data Our IT system is protected by technical organisational measures against unauthorised or unlawful processing by unauthorised people and against the accidental loss, destruction or damaging of the personal data.

RIGHT TO INSPECTION

Customers and partners have the right to inspect data concerning them and use their other rights defined in the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) (GDPR). Questions related to the matter need to be addressed to the controller’s contact person by email at marketing@ meka.eu. The inspection request must be sent as a signed email message. The data can also be inspected by visiting Unipro Oy Ltds head office. Visitors are required to provide a proof of their identity.

RIGHT TO REQUEST THE RECTIFICATION OF DATA

Customers and partners have the right to request the rectification of inaccurate data and use their other rights defined in the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) (GDPR). Questions related to the matter need to be addressed to the controller’s contact person by email at marketing@meka.eu. The request must be sent as a signed email message. The data can also be inspected by visiting Unipro Oy Ltd’s head office. Visitors are required to provide a proof of their identity.
