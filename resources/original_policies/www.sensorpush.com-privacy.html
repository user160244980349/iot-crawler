What information do the Products collect?

The Products collect:

  • Setup information you provide

  • Environmental data from the Products

  • Technical information from the Products

Environmental data from Products: We collect data from several sensors built into the Products. These sensors collect data such as current temperature and humidity.

Technical information from the Products: We record your Product model and serial number, Software version, and technical information.

Product data is stored locally on your mobile device and is not transmitted to SensorPush. Product data is only transmitted to SensorPush if you utilize the Services, as further detailed below.

What information does the SensorPush Gateway collect?

Product Usage information: If you have a SensorPush Gateway account or connected mobile device, we record adjustments you make to the Product through the Apps. We store this data along with your email address, information about your mobile device, data collected directly by the Product, a history of your Product settings, and any other information we have collected about your use of the Products and Services.

Setup information you provide: When you set up the SensorPush Gateway, you’ll be asked several questions in order to help us create an account. Such information includes your email address, password (for clarity it is not stored on our server in plaintext; we cannot see the original password), Product ID, Product Name (set by user), account creation time, and last time of most recent login.

Environmental data from the Products: We collect data from several sensors built into the Product. These sensors collect data such as current temperature and humidity. For example, this information includes Product alert settings (min/max temperature and humidity, if set by user), Product calibration offsets (if set by user), temperature and humidity data streams.

Technical information from the Product: We record your Product model and serial number, software version, and technical information. This information includes Product ID, Product name (set by user), Product token from mobile device for push notifications (designed by Apple to not be identifiable across application and installation), Product name (created by user, e.g. Wine Cellar) mobile device identification.

What additional information does SensorPush Gateway collect and store when a user connects a Product to the Internet or creates an account?

Wi-Fi Network Information: To connect the Product to our Services, or to access your Product over the Internet from a computer, a smartphone or a tablet, you will need to connect the Product to your Wi-Fi network. During setup, the Product will ask for your Wi-Fi network name (SSID) and password to connect to the Internet. It will save this information on the device, along with your IP address, so that you can access it and control it from your computer, smartphone or tablet, and so that the Product can communicate with SensorPush servers and download Software updates.

Once connected to your Wi-Fi network, the Product regularly sends the data described in this Privacy Policy (excluding your Wi-Fi password) to SensorPush to provide you with the Services.

Additional Authorized Users: SensorPush may provide you with the ability to enable additional authorized users to access your SensorPush Gateway account. If you do so, the additional authorized users may access the data from your Product. Invited users may also add additional authorized users to your account, so be careful to add only those you trust.

Email Addresses: When you create a SensorPush account, we collect and store your email address. From that point forward, your email address is used for communications to you by SensorPush. In addition, SensorPush may provide you with the ability to enable individuals to access your account as additional authorized users, or to invite other individuals such as friends or family to share access to your Product data.

Basic Profile Information: Your account allows you to provide certain basic profile information like your name and email address.

Bluetooth data:

The Products are Bluetooth enabled and may broadcast an identifying signal wirelessly. This is used to connect with your Bluetooth-enabled devices (such as a smartphone or tablet).

We provide support services and a Limited Warranty via live chat on our Sites, email request submissions, outbound telephone calls and direct email. We may use and process your personal information to handle your support request and provide you with the best support possible.
